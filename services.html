Process Guide

How the Buyback Program Works

Get paid for your read books in three quick steps.

1

Bring the Books

Visit our physical store in Bardoli with the books you have finished. We accept manga, textbooks, fanfiction prints, and biographies in readable condition.

2

Evaluation

Magan Lal inspects the physical binding, paper quality, and page integrity. We evaluate market demand to offer you the best possible valuation.

3

Instant Credit or Cash

Receive cash instantly, or opt for store credits. Store credits yield a higher value, which can be applied to any book in our store.

Affordable Reading

How Our Rental Service Works

Rent textbooks or manga on an affordable per-day basis.

1

Pick Your Titles

Browse our catalog on the Book Wiki or visit the store. Choose the manga volume, biography, or exam study guide you need.

2

Deposit & Daily Fee

Provide a small security deposit (fully refundable) and a modest per-day rental fee. Perfect for quick reads or study sessions.

3

Return & Refund

Once read, return the book in the same condition. We immediately return your security deposit, minus the total accumulated daily rental fees.
