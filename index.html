Buy-Back & Refurbishing Cycle

Don't let your finished books collect dust. Bring them to Tweakbooks! Owner Magan Lal buys back books in any condition. Our refurbishment process restores them:

  • Page cleaning and edge polishing.
  • Binding reinforcement and cover repair.
  • Re-sold at 40-60% lower prices, saving your wallet and the planet.
