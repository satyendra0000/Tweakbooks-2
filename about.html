Our Story

Nearly Three Decades of Local Literary Service

Tweakbooks was born from a simple mission: to bridge the gap between avid readers in Bardoli and global books. Under the care of Magan Lal, it has grown into a community pillar that stands for reading accessibility, affordability, and ecological responsibility.

ML

Magan Lal

Founder & Owner

Serving readers in Bardoli since 1996. Dedicated to sustainability, educational access, and sharing the joy of reading.

A Message from Magan Lal

"In 1996, the internet wasn't what it is today. Sourcing books in small towns like Bardoli was difficult. I opened Tweakbooks to serve local students and book lovers. Over time, I noticed how many high-quality books were thrown away once read. That led to our buyback and repair program.

Today, our store is more than a bookstore; it's a recycling center for ideas. We repair pages and clean bindings. We rent guides for competitive exams. My promise is that anyone who walks into Tweakbooks leaves with their next great read, regardless of their budget."

Values that Guide Us

How we build and support our reading community in Bardoli, Gujarat.

Reading Accessibility

Everyone deserves access to literature. By supplying used copies and rental plans, we ensure price is never a barrier to reading and learning.

Community-First Mindset

We support students during stressful exam periods, curate specialized manga for young readers, and recommend books to regular customers who have visited for years.

Ecological Recycling

A physical book should never be thrown away. Our refurbishing process extends the life of books, promoting sustainable recycling and helping the environment.

Milestones of Trust

How Tweakbooks has grown and adapted to serve Bardoli over the years.

1996

1996 - Store Foundation

Store Foundation

Magan Lal opened Tweakbooks Bookstore in Bardoli as a tiny book stall near Station Road to help local students source guides and exam prep books.

2005

2005 - Buyback Program Introduced

Buyback Program Introduced

To help readers manage book costs, we started buying back used copies from students, building a catalog of second-hand books.

2012

2012 - Launch of Book Rentals

Launch of Book Rentals

We launched our per-day renting service to assist students and light readers who wanted access to books without high purchase costs.

2018

2018 - Book Refurbishing Initiative

Book Refurbishing Initiative

We added book cleaning and binding equipment to restore damaged, pre-loved books, supporting circular reading and saving books from landfill.

2026

2026 - 29+ Years & Book Wiki Launch

29+ Years & Book Wiki Launch

Celebrating nearly three decades of service in Bardoli. We launched our digital Book Wiki search portal, helping readers check stocked titles before visiting.
